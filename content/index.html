Welcome Back, Commander

OpenRA is an Libre/Free (Open Source) Real Time Strategy game engine which recreates the look and gameplay of the first generation Command & Conquer games.

OpenRA currently includes mods reimagining C&C (Tiberian Dawn), C&C: Red Alert, and Dune 2000.

Cross-platform multiplayer is supported between Windows, OS X, and Linux, and singleplayer skirmishes are available vs bots.
